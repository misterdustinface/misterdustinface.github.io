Put the MathBox and the "mathwords.txt" in the same location. The MathBox will grab the "mathwords" text file and scan it. This will allow you to use a set of predefined keywords, and those keywords will be converted into other words or characters. I called this "MathBox" because I used it to make my math homework easier to write on a computer.
